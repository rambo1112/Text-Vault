Preparation & Logistics

The success of an 8-10 year expedition rests on a foundation of meticulous preparation. This section details the critical components of pre-expedition training, ongoing logistical management, and the specialized, modular gear system required to survive the planet's most extreme environments.

Physical Conditioning

  • Endurance: Progressively build to 40-50 km/day with a full pack.
  • Strength: Focus on core, legs, and back for injury prevention.
  • Acclimatization: Train in mountain, cold, and hot/humid conditions.

Skill Acquisition

  • Advanced Wilderness First Aid & Trauma Care certification.
  • Survival courses for Arctic, jungle, and desert environments.
  • Mastery of GPS, satellite imagery, and traditional navigation.
  • Basic mechanics for comprehensive gear repair.

Communication & Safety

  • Primary and backup satellite phones for daily check-ins.
  • Comprehensive medical kit for remote trauma and illness.
  • Emergency satellite consultation protocol with a doctor.
  • Air evacuation insurance plan.
